## Day-by-Day Itinerary

### Day 1: Sohar, Oman ‚Üí Doha, Qatar
- **Distance:** ~650 km
- **Driving Time:** 8 hours
- **Activities:** Travel day, arrive in Doha evening
- **Overnight:** Doha, Qatar

### Days 2-3: Exploring Doha, Qatar
**Recommended Places:**
1. **The Pearl-Qatar** - Luxury artificial island with marina walkways, boutiques, and cafes
2. **Museum of Islamic Art** - Stunning I.M. Pei architecture with world-class Islamic art collection
3. **Souq Waqif** - Traditional marketplace for spices, textiles, and authentic Qatari cuisine
4. **Katara Cultural Village** - Art galleries, theaters, and Mediterranean-style waterfront

**Overnight:** Doha, Qatar

### Day 4: Doha ‚Üí Al-Ahsa, Saudi Arabia
- **Distance:** ~300 km
- **Driving Time:** 3 hours
- **Border Crossing:** Qatar-Saudi Arabia
- **Activities:** Arrive Al-Ahsa afternoon, initial exploration
- **Overnight:** Al-Ahsa, Saudi Arabia

### Day 5: Al-Ahsa Exploration
**Recommended Places:**
1. **Al-Ahsa Oasis** - UNESCO World Heritage site, world's largest oasis with date palm gardens
2. **Qasr Ibrahim (Ibrahim Palace)** - Historic Ottoman fort built in 1571

**Overnight:** Al-Ahsa, Saudi Arabia

### Day 6: Al-Ahsa ‚Üí Kuwait City, Kuwait
- **Distance:** ~400 km
- **Driving Time:** 4-5 hours
- **Border Crossing:** Saudi Arabia-Kuwait
- **Activities:** Arrive Kuwait City afternoon
- **Overnight:** Kuwait City, Kuwait

### Day 7: Kuwait City Exploration
**Recommended Places:**
1. **Kuwait Towers** - Iconic landmark with observation deck and revolving restaurant
2. **The Avenues Mall** - One of Middle East's largest malls with 800+ stores

**Overnight:** Kuwait City, Kuwait

### Day 8: Kuwait City ‚Üí Hail, Saudi Arabia
- **Distance:** ~700 km
- **Driving Time:** 7-8 hours
- **Border Crossing:** Kuwait-Saudi Arabia
- **Activities:** Long drive day, arrive Hail evening
- **Overnight:** Hail, Saudi Arabia

### Day 9: Hail Exploration
**Recommended Places:**
1. **Jubbah Rock Art** - UNESCO World Heritage site with 10,000-year-old rock carvings
2. **Qishlah Palace** - Historic mud-brick palace with traditional Najdi architecture

**Overnight:** Hail, Saudi Arabia

### Day 10: Hail ‚Üí Riyadh ‚Üí Sohar, Oman
- **Distance:** ~650 km (Hail to Riyadh), then ~1,000 km (Riyadh to Sohar)
- **Total Driving Time:** ~12-14 hours (split driving recommended)
- **Option 1:** Quick Riyadh stop (2-3 hours) then drive to Oman
- **Option 2:** Overnight in Riyadh, return to Sohar on Day 11

**Riyadh Quick Stops (if time permits):**
1. **Masmak Fortress** - Historic fort showcasing Saudi Arabia's unification
2. **Kingdom Centre Tower** - 99-floor skyscraper with sky bridge views
3. **Diriyah Historic District** - UNESCO site, birthplace of first Saudi state

**End:** Return to Sohar, Oman

## Travel Tips

### Visa Requirements
- **Qatar:** eVisa available for Omani residents
- **Saudi Arabia:** eVisa or GCC resident visa
- **Kuwait:** eVisa available for GCC visitors
- Check current requirements before travel

### Border Crossings
1. **Qatar-Saudi (Abu Samra/Salwa)** - Busiest crossing, allow 1-2 hours
2. **Saudi-Kuwait (Nuwaiseeb)** - Smoother crossing, 30-60 minutes
3. **Saudi-Oman (Bat/Wadi Al Jizzi)** - Main crossing, 1-2 hours

### Driving Essentials
- Valid international driving permit or GCC license
- Vehicle registration and insurance (GCC coverage)
- Salik/Toll cards for Qatar and UAE
- Emergency kit and spare tire
- GPS/offline maps downloaded
- Fuel up before long stretches

### Best Time to Travel
- **December-March:** Ideal weather (15-25¬∞C)
- **Avoid:** June-August (extreme heat 40-50¬∞C)

### Accommodation Recommendations
- **Doha:** Pearl area or West Bay
- **Al-Ahsa:** Heritage hotels near the oasis
- **Kuwait City:** Salmiya or downtown areas
- **Hail:** City center hotels
- Book in advance, especially weekends

### Budget Estimate (Per Person)
- **Fuel:** $150-200
- **Accommodation:** $300-500 (budget to mid-range)
- **Food:** $200-300
- **Attractions:** $50-100
- **Misc:** $100
- **Total:** $800-1,200

### Currencies
- Qatar: Qatari Riyal (QAR) - 1 QAR ‚âà 0.27 USD
- Saudi Arabia: Saudi Riyal (SAR) - 1 SAR ‚âà 0.27 USD
- Kuwait: Kuwaiti Dinar (KWD) - 1 KWD ‚âà 3.25 USD (highest valued currency)

### Emergency Contacts
- **Qatar:** 999 (Police/Ambulance/Fire)
- **Saudi Arabia:** 997 (Police), 998 (Ambulance)
- **Kuwait:** 112 (All emergencies)

### Cultural Notes
- Respect local customs and dress codes
- Friday is the weekly holiday (some places closed)
- Ramadan: Eating in public during fasting hours restricted
- Photography: Ask permission before photographing people
