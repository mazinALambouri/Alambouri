## How to Add Places to Your Trip

### Method 1: Create Your Own Custom Place

1. Click on any **DAY** tab
2. Click **"+ Add More Places"** button
3. Click **"+ Create Your Own Place"** (green button)
4. Fill in the form:
   - **Place Name** (required) - e.g., "Al Baik Restaurant"
   - **Description** - What's special about this place
   - **Location** - City or area name
   - **Price** - Cost in your chosen currency
   - **Currency** - QAR, SAR, KWD, or OMR
   - **Time Category** - When you'll visit (Breakfast, Visit, Prayer, Lunch, Activity, Dinner, Hotel)
   - **Image URL** (optional) - Link to an image
5. Click **"Add Custom Place"**
6. Your custom place appears in the timeline!

## Tips for Custom Places

### Images
- Use direct image URLs from Unsplash, Google Images, or your own hosting
- Format: `https://example.com/image.jpg`
- If you don't add an image, a default scenic photo is used

### Pricing
- Enter 0 for free places
- Use local currency (OMR for Oman, SAR for Saudi Arabia, etc.)

### Time Categories
Choose the right category for timeline organization:
- **Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner** - Restaurants and meals
- **Visit** - Attractions, landmarks, shopping, beaches
- **Prayer** - Mosques and prayer locations
- **Activity** - Sports, adventures, events
- **Hotel** - Where you'll sleep

## Timeline Display

Your custom places appear in the timeline just like recommended places:
- Numbered sequence (1, 2, 3...)
- Color-coded badge showing time category
- Your entered details (name, location, price, description)
- Images displayed in grid

## Editing Custom Places

Currently, to edit a custom place:
1. Delete it using the trash icon
2. Create a new one with updated details
